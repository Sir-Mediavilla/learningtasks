ABOUT US

HFTP (Help For The Philippines) is a non-profit non-governmental organization that distributes donations to trusted organizations that have made a large impact on combating poverty and utilizing them in personal programs to also help the less fortunate. We are a versatile organization that can provide help for different problems within a community, whether it be for cleanup or to provide supplies, HFTP is always ready to help.
