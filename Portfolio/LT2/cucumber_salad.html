Cucumber Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 sliced Cucumber
  • 3 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh
  • 8 ounces Lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Sour cream
  • 1/2 pinch of Salt
  • 1 teaspoon dill

Methods

    1) Dissolve sugar in lemon juice.

    2) Add sour cream, salt and dill.

    3) Mix with the cucumbers.
